What is AthenaUI?

AthenaUI is a next‑generation agentic AI platform designed to give users full control, transparency, and customization over their AI workflows. Built with modularity at its core, AthenaUI adapts to your needs — whether you're building automations, managing content, or developing advanced AI‑driven systems.

With AthenaUI, you’re not just using AI. You’re shaping it.

AthenaNavigator

AthenaNavigator is your command center — a visual, intuitive interface that lets you build, modify, and deploy AI workflows with ease. Drag‑and‑drop modules, connect logic, and watch your automations come to life.

Athena Code Studio

For developers and power users, Athena Code Studio provides a full scripting environment where you can write custom logic, extend AthenaUI’s capabilities, and integrate external APIs. Build your own Amazing programs, Vibe Coding taken to the Next Level!

Security & Transparency

AthenaUI is built with user control at the forefront. No hidden processes. No opaque decisions. You see what your AI is doing — and why.

AI Governance Built Into the Core

AthenaUI is designed with governance at its foundation. As multi‑agent systems grow more capable, the need for transparent, auditable, and user‑controlled AI becomes essential — not just in v2.0, but in every version that follows.

Every action taken by an agent passes through a permission layer, ensuring you stay in control. All decisions are logged with signed audit trails, allowing you to trace workflows and verify behavior.

With identity‑locked agents, user‑controlled capabilities, and a privacy‑first troubleshooting system, AthenaUI ensures autonomy never comes at the cost of oversight. Governance isn’t a limitation — it’s the framework that lets you build faster and safer.

Customization

Every part of AthenaUI is customizable — from agent behavior, to workflow logic, to interface layout, to memory preferences, to automation triggers. AthenaUI adapts to your style, your needs, and your goals.
