What is AthenaUI?

AthenaUI is a next‑generation agentic AI platform designed to give users full control, transparency, and customization over their AI workflows. Built with modularity at its core, AthenaUI adapts to your needs — whether you're building automations, managing content, or developing advanced AI‑driven systems.

With AthenaUI, you’re not just using AI. You’re shaping it.

AthenaNavigator

AthenaNavigator is your command center — a visual, intuitive interface that lets you build, modify, and deploy AI workflows with ease. Drag‑and‑drop modules, connect logic, and watch your automations come to life.

Athena Code Studio

For developers and power users, Athena Code Studio provides a full scripting environment where you can write custom logic, extend AthenaUI’s capabilities, and integrate external APIs.

Security & Transparency

AthenaUI is built with user control at the forefront. No hidden processes. No opaque decisions. You see what your AI is doing — and why.

Customization

Every module, every workflow, every agent can be customized. AthenaUI adapts to your style, your needs, and your goals.
